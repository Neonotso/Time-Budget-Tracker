## Energy Type Key
- **Focused**: Requires concentration, minimal distractions
- **Creative**: Requires inspiration and creative flow
- **Physical**: Requires bodily energy and movement
- **Social**: Involves interaction with others
- **Learning**: Requires mental absorption of new information
- **Inspired**: Requires intrinsic motivation and passion
- **Organizing**: Requires systematic thinking and categorization
- **Routine**: Habit-based, can be done with lower energy
- **Problem-solving**: Requires analytical thinking and troubleshooting
